Play online Help

FICS: Free Internet Chess Server let's you play with thousands of chess players online.
Warning: if your device switches from one internet access point to another, the connection can break. There is no timeseal.

Menu

Challenges
Who is seeking a match. Click on the player in the list to accept a challenge.
Seek
Set up your own challenge. If you fill in the handle of a player you know, it will make a direct challenge.
Games
Watch games being played online. A grandmaster can be identified by the GM prefix to the handle.

Buttons

The three on-screen buttons can be used to:
- add a prefix to text you enter to switch between a custom command or chat
- access to a list of usefull functions
- open a large text console
